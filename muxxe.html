Muxxe Brand Identity

Muxxe


Muxxe is a jewelry company with the sole purpose of helping people heal and grow through the power of gemstones and crystals.
Their brand identity displays the modern side of the brand while at the same time being elegant and keeping the element of the stones withtin the logo.
